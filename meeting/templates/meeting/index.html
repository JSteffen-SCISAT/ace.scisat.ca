    Ace Meeting

    Joint ACE-Odin Meeting

    Oct 23-25, 2017

    • Getting to UWaterloo -
    • Transportation -
    • Accommodations, Restaurants, and Attractions -
    • Parking

    The next ACE Meeting will be a joint ACE-Odin Meeting, held in the Davis Centre at the University of Waterloo in Waterloo, Ontario. This meeting will focus primarily on the achievements, current work, and future plans of the ACE (SCISAT) and Odin satellite missions. In addition to the usual speakers at ACE and Odin meetings, speakers from other missions, space agencies, modelling groups, and validation teams are invited to participate. Potential new users of ACE and Odin data are particularly encouraged to attend.

    • Location: Davis Centre (DC) 1304, University of Waterloo
    • Time: 9 - 5 PM
    • Address:
      200 University Avenue West
